CVC5: Comprehensive Spelling Lesson

Spelling Exercise

Selected Words for Spelling:
  • Cat
  • Hen
  • Pin
  • Top
  • Gum
  • Wax
  • Bed
Dictation:
  • The cat sat on the bed.
  • A hen and a wax pin.
  • Gum is on top of the pot.
Activity: Dictation where each word is spoken, and students are required to spell them correctly.
