CVC2: Short 'a' Vowel Sound

Reading Activity

Words: Cat, Bat, Map, Van, Jam, Wax, Rat
Example sentences:
  • The cat and the bat sat on the mat.
  • Sam has a map and a van full of jam.
  • Max drips wax on a rat.
Activity: Students read sentences using these words.

Spelling Activity

Words: Cap, Map, Hat, Lag, Tan, Pad, Nap
Activity: Dictation of words. Students spell each out loud after hearing them.
