CVC3: Short 'a' and 'i' Vowel Sounds

Reading Exercise

Words: (Short 'a' from CVC2) Cat, Bat, Van, Map + (Short 'i') Sit, Fit, Bin, Pin
Example sentences:
  • The bat and the cat sit on a pin.
  • Fit the map into the bin.
Activity: Students read sentences incorporating both sets of words.

Spelling Exercise

Words: (Short 'a' from CVC2) Cap, Map, Bat, Van + (Short 'i') Sit, Fit, Bin, Pin
Activity: Students spell the words after hearing them read aloud. This helps reinforce both vowel sounds.
