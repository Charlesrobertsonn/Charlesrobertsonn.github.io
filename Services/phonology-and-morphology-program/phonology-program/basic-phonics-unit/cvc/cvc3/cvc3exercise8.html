CVC3 - Short 'o' Spelling Activity

Activity: Listen to the sentences and then write them down.

Sentences to Write:

1. "A fox on the box naps."

2. "Don logs into the pod and sits."

3. "Tod drops the rod."

4. "Lob the cob into the mob."

5. "A dog hops on a log and a cat naps in a bag."

6. "Rob got a pot and Tim lit it."
