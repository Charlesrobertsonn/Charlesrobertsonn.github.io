CVC4: Short 'a', 'i', and 'o' Vowel Sounds

Reading Exercise

Words: (Short 'a' from CVC2) Cat, Map + (Short 'i' from CVC3) Sit, Pin + (Short 'o') Pot, Top, Dot, Cob
Example sentences:
  • The cat sits on top of the pot.
  • Pin the map on the cob.
Activity: Students read sentences that include these words.

Spelling Exercise

Words: (Short 'a' from CVC2) Cat, Map + (Short 'i' from CVC3) Sit, Pin + (Short 'o') Pot, Top
Activity: Dictation where each word is spoken, and students are required to spell them correctly.
