Phonology and Morphology Programs

Welcome to our dedicated page for Phonology and Morphology educational resources. Click on each section to learn more about our specialized programs.

Phonology

Phonology involves the study of sound systems within a language, focusing on how sounds function within a given language or languages. This program offers structured lessons to enhance phonemic awareness and pronunciation skills.

Explore Phonology Program

Morphology

Morphology deals with the study of words, how they are formed, and their relationship to other words in the same language. Our program covers the structure of words including stems, root words, prefixes, and suffixes.
