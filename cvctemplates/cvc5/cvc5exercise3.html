CVC5: Comprehensive Reading Exercise

Reading Exercise

Words:
  • Cat
  • Sit
  • Pot
  • Bed
  • Cub
  • Map
  • Pin
  • Top
  • Hen
  • Gum
  • Rod
  • Wax
Sentences for Reading:
  • The cat and the hen sit on the bed.
  • The cub plays with a pin on top of the map.
  • Put the pot on the gum and the wax on the rod.
Activity: Students read sentences that include these words.
