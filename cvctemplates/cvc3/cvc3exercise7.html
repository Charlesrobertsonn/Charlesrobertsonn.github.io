CVC3 - Short 'o', 'i', and 'a' Reading Activity

Activity: Read the words and sentences aloud to practice the short 'o', 'i', and 'a' sounds.

Words:

  • Cop
  • Hop
  • Mop
  • Pop
  • Top
  • Sop
  • Hip
  • Rip
  • Mat
  • Bat

Sentences:

  • "Tom mops the shop."
  • "Bill hits a bat."
  • "Jen hops on the top."
  • "The cat hops on a cop."
