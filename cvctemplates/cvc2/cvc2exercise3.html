CVC1: Short 'a' Vowel Sound

Reading Exercise

Words: Cat, Bat, Mad, Jam, Wax
Example sentences:
  • The cat sat on the mat.
  • Sam has a bat and a red cap.
Activity: Display sentences using these words and have students read aloud.

Spelling Exercise

Words: Cap, Map, Pat, Tag, Wag
Activity: Say the words aloud and have students write them down on paper. For an interactive online approach, use the virtual whiteboard below where students can type their answers.
