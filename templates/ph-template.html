Lesson Title

Introduction

Welcome to the lesson on phonology. This lesson consists of three stages: Revision, Learning, and Reinforcement. Each stage has three exercises.

Stage 1: Revision

  • Exercise 1: Sound cards
  • Exercise 2: Syllables
  • Exercise 3: Spelling

Stage 2: Learning

  • Exercise 1: New Phonic - Intro/Reading
  • Exercise 2: New Phonic Spelling
  • Exercise 3: New Syllable - Reading

Stage 3: Reinforcement

  • Exercise 1: Spelling only New Phonic and New Syllable
  • Exercise 2: Writing Sentences
  • Exercise 3: Reading a text
